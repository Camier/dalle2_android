PRIVACY POLICY FOR DALL-E AI ART GENERATOR

Last Updated: January 2025
Version: 1.0.0

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to DALL-E AI Art Generator ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide
- API Keys: Your OpenAI API key (stored securely and encrypted)
- User Preferences: App settings and customization choices
- Generated Content: Images you create and their prompts
- Age Information: Birth date for age verification (not stored after verification)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device Information: Device type, operating system version
- App Usage Data: Features used, generation frequency (only if analytics consent given)
- Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes (only if consent given)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:
- Provide core app functionality (image generation)
- Store your preferences and settings
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Ensure age-appropriate access
- Comply with legal obligations

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- All sensitive data (API keys) are encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- Data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- API communications go directly to OpenAI's servers

5. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

5.1 Consent Management
You can manage your privacy preferences at any time:
- Essential Services: Required for app functionality
- Analytics & Performance: Optional, helps improve the app
- Crash Reports: Optional, helps identify and fix issues
- Personalization: Optional, enhances your experience

5.2 Data Rights (GDPR Compliance)
You have the right to:
- Access: Export all your data
- Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
- Erasure: Delete all your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
- Object: Opt-out of optional data processing

5.3 Age Restrictions
- Minimum age: 13 years (16 in EU countries)
- Age verification required before first use
- Parental consent may be required in some jurisdictions

6. DATA RETENTION

- Active data: Retained while you use the app
- Default retention: 365 days after last use
- You can request immediate deletion at any time
- Audit logs: Anonymized and retained for legal compliance

7. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

This app uses OpenAI's DALL-E API:
- Your prompts and generated images are processed by OpenAI
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies to their services
- We do not share your data with any other third parties

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (16 in EU)
- Age verification is required
- If we discover underage usage, associated data will be deleted

9. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

- We will cooperate with relevant authorities
